Two Cup Of Tea

Recipe for making tea!!

Ingredients

  • Water-1 cup (250 ml)
  • Milk-1 cup (250 ml)
  • Tea leaves-2 teaspoons (adjust to taste)
  • Sugar-2 teaspoons (or as per taste)
  • Ginger- ½ inch piece (optional,crushed)
  • Cardamom-1 pod (optional, crused)

Instructions

  1. Boil water

    • Pour 1 cup of water into a saucepan.
    • Place it on medium heat and let it start boiling.

  2. Add tea leaves and spices

    • Add 2 teaspoons of tea leaves.
    • Add crushed ginger and cardamom (if using).
    • Let it boil for about 2–3 minutes so the flavors mix well.

  3. Add milk

    • Pour in 1 cup of milk.
    • Stir well and allow the tea to boil again.

  4. Add sugar

    • Add 2 teaspoons of sugar (or as per taste).
    • Mix well.

  5. Simmer the tea

    • Lower the heat slightly.
    • Let the tea boil for 3–4 minutes until it gets a nice brown color and strong aroma
    • You can boil longer for stronger tea or less for lighter tea.

  6. Strain and serve

    • Turn off the gas.
    • Use a strainer to pour the tea into cups.

Enjoy your hit cuo of tea!

You can serve it with biscuits or snacks like samosas or pakoras.
